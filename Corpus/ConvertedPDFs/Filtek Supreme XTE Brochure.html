Take aesthetics to the next level with a dual-shade
technique. It’s simpler than you might think.
You may already know the benefits of working with Filtek™ Supreme XTE Universal Restorative.
What you may not know is how easy it is to move from a single-shade to a dual-shade technique.
Filtek Supreme XTE restorative syringes and capsules are colour-coded by opacity and have bold
lettering for fast, accurate selection. Plus, 3M Oral Care has many resources to guide you, like
an interactive shade selector wheel, step-by-step technique cards, brochure and videos.
Find them at: www.filteksupremexte.com.au under the Downloads & Resources tab
or ask your 3M Oral Care Product Specialist.
